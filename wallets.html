Store and send FTM and other assets

Safely store FTM and Fantom-based tokens, including fUSDT and USDC. Fantom is fully compatible with Metamask, Ledger and the leading mobile wallets.

Explore wallets

fWallet is Fantom Official wallet. With fWallet you send, receive, stake your FTM and access Fantom DeFi ecosystem.

Access fWallet How to use fWallet

Metamask is the most popular software wallet and browser extension. Metamask allows you to store mainnet FTM and interact with Fantom dApps.

Download Metamask Set up Metamask on Fantom

Ledger Nano is the most popular hardware wallet and the most secure way to store your mainnet FTM and interact with dApps on Fantom.

Buy Ledger wallet How to use Ledger Nano S / X on Fantom

Coinbase Wallet is the wallet of the world’s biggest crypto exchange. More than 1 million users can easily store FTM and access the Fantom network.

Download Coinbase Wallet

Trust Wallet is the most trusted and secure crypto wallet, used by 10 million+ people. Trust Wallet supports mainnet FTM.

Download Trust Wallet

MathWallet is a multi-platform universal crypto wallet. MathWallet supports mainnet FTM and is available for mobile, browser, web, and hardware.

Download MathWallet

Coin98 Wallet a non-custodial, multi-chain wallet and DeFi gateway to store your mainnet FTM. Coin98 is available for iOS and Android.

Download Coin98 wallet

Edge is a powerful and easy to use cryptocurrency wallet that allows you to store your mainnet FTM. Edge is available for iOS and Android.

Download Edge

BitKeep is a multi-chain wallet with a focus on DeFi that allows you store you mainnet FTM. BitKeep is available for iOS and Android.

Download BitKeep

OKEx wallet is a multichain browser extension and mobile wallet for iOS and Android. OKEx wallet supports mainnet FTM.

Get OKEx Wallet

D’CENT is a Biometric wallet, Card type wallet, and Software wallet that can all be managed from a single mobile app. D’CENT supports mainnet FTM.

Download D'CENT wallet

TokenPocket is a self-custodial wallet, which supports over 20 mainstream public chains including Fantom.

Download TokenPocket

Bridge wallet is an on-ramp, off-ramp wallet. It allows you to buy FTM & USDC with the lowest fees, swap on SpookySwap, and cash out in minutes.

Download bridge

SimpleHold is a non-custodial wallet that supports over 200 well-known cryptocurrencies, including FTM. It is available as an extension and mobile app.

Download SimpleHold

Shield is a Security Tool, MultiChain Wallet, MultiChain Bridge, and First 2FA on blockchain. It is available for Android & iOS Mobile.

Get SHIELD Wallet

Krystal Wallet is a non-custodial, multi-chain wallet which you can use to access the Fantom DeFi ecosystem. Send, receive, swap mainnet FTM and more.

Access Krystal

Huobi Wallet is a secure and user-friendly wallet that seamlessly connects to dApps across all major blockchains.

Download Huobi Wallet
